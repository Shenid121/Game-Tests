How To Play

Teams alternate turns to pick items that match the shown category.

  • Correct pick: +1 point and streak +1.
  • Wrong pick: no point and streak resets to 0.
  • Streak bonus: streak 3+ gives +2 points, streak 5+ gives +3 points.
  • If timer runs out, that team loses streak and turn switches.
  • Classic mode chooses a random category each turn.
  • Speedrun mode uses phones only, both teams score for 60 seconds at the same time.

Voice mode tips: say the visible item name like "lion" or "moss", or say the item number like "one", "two", "three", or "four". You can also say "help", "stop listening", and "start listening".
